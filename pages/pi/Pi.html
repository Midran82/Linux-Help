Raspberry Pi

*The Raspberry Pi is a credit-card-sized single-board computer developed in the UK by the Raspberry Pi Foundation with the intention of promoting the teaching of basic computer science in schools. *wikipedia

The Raspberry Pi also comes in two different "setups", Model A and Model B, with the Model B slightly more advanced and has a higher price.

*Some specs

Model: Model B Model A
Price: US 25$** US 35$**
CPU: 700MHz 700MHz
Memory(SDRAM): 256MB 512MB
USB 2.0 ports: 1 port 2 port
Network: None 10/100 Mbit/s Ethernet
Power Rating: 300 mA (1.5W) 700 mA (3.5W)
Weight: 45 g (1.6 oz) 45 g (1.6 oz)

*wikipedia
** prices may differ from vendors and country, but it shows the difference between the two models.

There is many different OS for the Raspberry Pi, but i would personally recommend using NOOBS that has multiple OS available on it and therefore a good place to start when you are not sure what to install.
