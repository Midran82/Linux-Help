Why install Linux?

Well, there is many factors to why you should install a Linux based operation system on your computer.
1. Your old operation system is outdated (Windows XP is not supported anymore).
2. Your computer is old and installing one of the newest Windows operation systems would be costly and not worth it, and might not even run very good (could buy a new computer instead).
3. You have many computers that need OS upgrades (Windows could be costly).
4. You just need a computer that has the basic of programs installed (Web-browser, Office programs, music/video playback).

Update/Upgrade OS

To keep your system up to date you should once in a while update and upgrade your system.
Step 1: Open your Terminal window.
Step 2: sudo apt-get update
Step 3: sudo apt-get upgrade
