Introduction

Why did i make this site? Well, after trying to dig into the Linux community and struggling to find out what kind of OS Distribution I should choose, how to install it, and generally how to manage going away from the well known Microsoft operation system Windows and into the Linux world.

I searched the web over and over again after useful things I would need to know to make it easier and found loads of different pages. This made it hard to keep track on everything and what pages had what. Therefore I decided to make this page, and put everything I had learned from different pages around the Internet on the same page. Some things I found out for myself others I had to really, really search for.

So I really hope this page will help others to find out the things they need and hopefully I can keep this page as up to date as possible in this fast changing environment.
